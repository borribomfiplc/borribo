# BORRIBO HRMS v39

- Added automatic sign-out after 5 continuous minutes without activity.
- Pointer, keyboard, scrolling, and touch activity reset the timer.
- Activity is synchronized across tabs so an inactive background tab does not sign out an actively used session.
- Applies to Admin, HR, Employee, and Kiosk sessions.
- Login shows a Khmer notice after an automatic sign-out.
- Remember Me can still remember the username, but cannot bypass the inactivity timeout.

# Borribo HRMS v34

Built from the user-provided v32 source so all employee-management actions remain available.

## Working schedule

- Monday-Friday: 08:00-17:00
- Saturday: 08:00-12:00
- Sunday: non-working day
- Late arrival and early departure are calculated from the attendance record's Cambodia weekday.
- The former morning/evening employee shift selector is replaced by one organization-wide schedule.

## Preserved v32 employee functions

- View employee details
- Edit employee information
- Mark an employee inactive without deleting historical data
- Employee actions and employment-history tracking

Existing attendance and employee records containing legacy shift labels remain readable.
